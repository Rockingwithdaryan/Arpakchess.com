Who is Arpak?

Champion. Competitor.
Chess Coach.

Arpak Worya Mohammed Qanee is a five-time BC Senior Chess Champion whose competitive career has made him one of the most respected figures in British Columbia's chess community. His results speak for themselves. Consistent excellence at the highest level of provincial play over many years.

Arpak started playing chess at a young age, quickly demonstrating a natural talent for the game. Over the years, he has won numerous tournaments and has been a fixture in the BC chess scene, known for his strategic mindset, shockingly brilliant plays and competitive spirit.

Now, Arpak is a chess coach on top of being a BC senior champion who's eager to share his knowledge and experience with the next generation of chess enthusiasts. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an advanced player seeking to refine your skills, Arpak offers coaching and mentorship that can help you reach your full potential.
