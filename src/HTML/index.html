About Arpak

A Champion
Born at the Board

Arpak Worya Mohammed Qanee discovered chess at a young age and quickly demonstrated a rare natural talent for the game. Growing up with an analytical mind and fierce competitive drive, he devoted himself to mastering every facet of chess — from sharp tactical play to deep positional understanding.

Over the years, Arpak has competed in tournaments across British Columbia and beyond, steadily climbing the ranks to claim the provincial senior championship not once, but four times. His consistency at the highest level of BC chess speaks to his discipline and relentless preparation.

Beyond the trophies, Arpak is passionate about growing the chess community. Whether mentoring newcomers or sharing insights with experienced players, he brings the same dedication to the board that has defined his entire career.
